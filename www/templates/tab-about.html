About

Macquarie University offers a free shuttle bus service around the Campus during semester. Simply hop on or off wherever you choose and use the service as much or as little as you like.

The shuttle bus is a complimentary service for all staff, students and guests. It operates on Monday to Friday during semester from 4:00pm to 12.30am, meaning you can use the service to transport you to and from Buildings, the Hub, train station and student accommodation.
